Under the Hood

Built on proven technology.

You don't need to know the tech details — but here's why this stack matters: it's fast, it scales, and it's what Shopify themselves recommend for custom storefronts.

⚡

Shopify Hydrogen

Shopify's own framework for custom storefronts. Lightning fast, full checkout integration, automatic tax + shipping.

🎨

Canvas2D Compositor

Real-time design rendering on product photos. Perspective warp + embroidery texture = realistic mockups.

☁

S3 + Shopify Metafields

Product data and templates stored in Shopify. Preview images on S3. No extra database needed for V1.

Deploys to Shopify Oxygen (or Vercel). Monitored with Sentry. Tested with Playwright visual regression.
